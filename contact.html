Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our bespoke joinery services and process

What is your design process?

We begin with a comprehensive consultation to understand your vision, followed by concept design, material selection, detailed planning, craftsmanship in our workshop, and professional installation. Each step involves close collaboration with you.

How long does a typical project take?

Timelines vary based on project complexity. Kitchen projects typically take 8-12 weeks from design to installation, while smaller projects like vanities may take 4-6 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during the consultation phase.

Do you offer a warranty?

Yes, all our work comes with a comprehensive 5-year craftsmanship warranty. We use premium materials and construction techniques to ensure longevity and durability that stands the test of time.

What areas do you serve?

We are based in Toronto and serve clients throughout the Greater Toronto Area. We also take on select projects across Ontario and other parts of Canada for larger residential and commercial joinery projects.

What is your pricing structure?

As a bespoke service, pricing varies based on project scope, materials, and complexity. We provide detailed quotes after the initial consultation. Our focus is on value and quality that justifies the investment.

How do I get started?

Begin by contacting us through this form, by phone, or email to schedule a complimentary consultation. We'll discuss your vision, assess your space, and provide initial guidance on possibilities and next steps.
